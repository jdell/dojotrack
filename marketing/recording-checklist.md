# EntrenaDojo — Video recording checklist

Step-by-step guide for recording 8 tutorials with Loom. Each takes 3-5 minutes.

## Before you start

### Setup (do once)
1. Install Loom: [loom.com/download](https://www.loom.com/download) (free tier = 25 videos, 5 min each)
2. Open EntrenaDojo in Chrome → log in as Club Mi Casita
3. Set the browser language to Spanish (most of your audience)
4. Use Chrome at **1280×720** or **1440×900** — clean resolution for video
5. Close all other tabs (no distracting favicons in the tab bar)
6. Turn off notifications (Do Not Disturb mode)
7. Have a glass of water nearby

### Recording settings (Loom)
- Screen + Camera (small circle with your face in the corner — adds trust)
- Audio: your microphone
- Screen: current tab only (not full screen — hides your desktop)
- HD quality ON

### General tips
- **Talk while you click** — narrate what you're doing as you do it
- **Pause 1 second** after each click before speaking — Loom AI will cut dead air
- **If you mess up, just keep going** — Loom lets you trim later
- **End each video with:** "Empieza gratis en entrenadojo.es"

| 6 | Point at the sidebar | "Desde aquí gestionas alumnos, clases, cinturones, pagos — todo." |

**Duration:** ~90 seconds. **File name:** `01-setup-club.mp4`

## Tutorial 4: "Check-in con QR"

**Goal:** Show the magic moment of QR attendance.

| Step | What to show on screen | What to say |
|------|----------------------|-------------|
| 1 | Click "Clases" in sidebar | "Vamos a ver cómo funciona el check-in automático." |
| 2 | Click on a class (e.g. "Fundamentals") | "Abrimos la clase de hoy." |
| 3 | Show the upcoming session with the QR code button | "Cada sesión tiene un código QR." |
| 4 | Click the QR code → show the full QR | "El alumno escanea este código con su teléfono." |
| 5 | **Switch to phone** (or show the /checkin URL in a new tab) | "Abre la cámara, escanea, y listo — registrado." |
| 6 | Go back to the session → show the check-in counter updated | "El contador se actualiza al instante." |
| 7 | Show a student profile with the attendance table | "Y la asistencia queda en el historial del alumno." |
| 8 | Pause | "Sin listas de papel. Empieza gratis en entrenadojo.es" |

**Duration:** ~90 seconds. **File name:** `04-qr-checkin.mp4`

**Tip:** For the phone scanning part, you can use your phone camera pointed at the screen showing the QR code. Or just open the check-in URL manually — the audience gets the idea.

## After recording

### Edit (2 min per video)
1. Open each video in Loom → trim the start/end
2. Loom AI auto-removes filler words and adds chapters
3. Add a title and description

### Create short clips (OpusClip, optional)
1. Download each Loom video
2. Upload to [opus.pro](https://opus.pro) (free: 60 min/month)
3. It auto-generates 5-10 vertical clips per video with captions
4. Download and post to Instagram Reels / TikTok / YouTube Shorts

### Post
- YouTube: all 8 as a playlist "EntrenaDojo — Tutoriales"
- Instagram: one Reel per day for 8 days (from OpusClip clips)
- WhatsApp Status: share one clip per day
- Landing page: embed the YouTube playlist
