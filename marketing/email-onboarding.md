Hi {{clubOwnerName}},

Your students are in. Now let's give them something to show up for.

Go to **Classes** and create your weekly schedule. For each class, set:

- Day and time
- Discipline and level (beginners, advanced, all levels)
- Instructor

Students scan it with their phone to check in. No calling names, no paper lists. You see attendance in real time on your dashboard, and it feeds directly into belt progression tracking.

Your schedule also shows up on your **public club page**, so prospective students can see when you train.

[Create Your Schedule]({{classesUrl}})

**Send timing:** Day 7
**Subject (EN):** Time to get paid
**Subject (ES):** Es hora de cobrar

### English

Hi {{clubOwnerName}},

You have been running {{clubName}} on EntrenaDojo for a week. Students are in, classes are scheduled. Now let's handle the awkward part -- money.

**Create a payment plan in under a minute:**

1. Go to **Payments**
2. Click **Create Plan** (e.g., "Monthly Training - 45 EUR")
3. Set the amount and frequency
4. Share the payment link with students via WhatsApp

Students pay with card through Stripe. Secure, instant, straight to your bank account.

**Not everyone pays online?** No problem. You can record cash and Bizum payments manually. Select the student, enter the amount, done. Your payment history stays complete either way.

No more chasing. No more "I'll pay you next week." Just a link, a tap, and it is handled.
